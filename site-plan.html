Overview

Name

The name selected represents that the website will be focused on providing opportunities for job hunting in Costa Rica

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to connect propspective employees in Costa Rica with job opportunities, recruiters and other talent development resources.

Scenarios

  • What are some job openings that match my profile?
  • Can I get my resume reviewed by a professional?
  • Can I find some freelancing opportunities in my area?
